# Wellness Disclaimer

HiAir provides informational wellness support based on environmental and user-provided data.

- HiAir is **not** a medical device.
- HiAir does **not** provide diagnosis or treatment.
- HiAir output must not be used as a substitute for professional medical advice.
- In urgent situations or emergencies, contact local emergency services immediately.

## In-App Wording Guardrails
- Avoid claims like "diagnosis", "cure", "treats", "emergency-safe".
- Use wellness-oriented language: "may help", "consider", "observe", "consult professionals if needed".
